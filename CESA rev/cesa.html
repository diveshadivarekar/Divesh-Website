ABOUT CESA

Key features of our organization

Computer department

CESA is a organization which comes under computer department. it organizes and manages all the events for the computer department.

Team work

CESA is a an group of student's organizing and managing events. We all work as a team so the importance of the team work is known.

Members

CESA is an organization composed of skill full individuals, which work together to organize workshops and events with their creative mindset and enthusiastic spirit.

Goals

The aim of our organization is to achieve the goals and objective through effective performance which is possible with the skills of our members and to conduct events which will be beneficial for the students and which will provide them great experience.
